Khashlama

Best dish in the world!

Khashlama is a boiled beef dish popular in the Kakheti region of Georgia and usually always part of a Kakhetian supra.

Ingredients

  • 500 gr. Beef
  • 2 onions
  • 3-4 Laurel leaves
  • 5 gr. Mixed peppercorn (red, black, and white suits great to each other)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground basil
  • 750 ml. White dry wine
  • 1/2 Bundle of coriander, parsley, celery
  • 1/2 Salt
  • 1 Ñ€inch Bundle of green onion

Steps

  1. Clean and wash your beef, cut into middle-sized cubes and throw in a pot. Cover it with 2/3 of water and place on a middle fire.
  2. Drain the first boiled broth of the meat and fill up the pot with some fresh water again. Then peel the onion and throw into the pot without cutting it, add peppercorns and ground basil.
  3. Avoid drying with periodically pouring small portions of water down into it. Thoroughly wash herbs, wrap them up in a foil and throw them in the meat pot after an hour of boiling. The broth will absorb its entire aroma and will get a beautiful smell and amazing taste. The final stage is adding some wine and boiling for 5 more minutes to let the alcohol vaporize. The time of cooking depends on the meat quality as the final product should be very soft. Salt it in the end.
  4. Serve hot together with the broth. You can also top with thinly sliced coriander and green onion. Enjoy with a glass of white dry wine.
