Kharcho

Kharcho with walnuts

Kharcho, also spelled as Harcho, is a traditional Georgian soup containing beef, rice, cherry plum pur√©e and chopped walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg. beef brisket
  • 2 onions
  • 1 kg. tomatoes
  • 20 gr. garlic
  • 20 gr. dried coriander
  • 30 gr. Utskho Suneli (Blue Fenugreek)
  • 350 gr. walnuts
  • 100 gr. butter
  • 50 ml. corn flour
  • 5 gr. salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and cut your beef into small pieces and stew in some water for about 25 minutes.
  2. Then add already blendered tomatoes. In 2-3 minutes give some more water to cover the meat and let it stew on a low fire.
  3. When the meat is cooked add your walnut mix. Cook them together for another 3-5 minutes and stir by. Add some salt and pounded garlic
